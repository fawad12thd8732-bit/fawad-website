QR Code Aadharit Ped Goapan aur Dekhbhaal

Ek Project by Fawad Ahmad

Samasya (Problem)

Ped lagane ke baad log unki dekhbhaal nahi karte, isse vo sookh jaate hain ya kaat diye jaate hain.

Samadhan (Solution)

Har ped par ek unique QR code chipkaya jaayega. Jab koi use scan karega, to us ped ki puri jankari milegi — jaise ki kisne lagaya, kab lagaya, kis prakaar ka ped hai, etc.

Kaise Kaam Karega?

  • Ped ke saath ek QR Code laga hoga
  • Scan karne par tree info show hogi
  • Janta ya school students ped ki dekhbhaal kar sakte hain
  • Har adoption ko digital system me track kiya jaayega

Fayde (Benefits)

  • Logon me ped bachane ki bhavna badegi
  • Monitoring system se har ped track hoga
  • Environment ke prati samvedansheelata badhegi
